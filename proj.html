Majhe Projects


JewelARy: An Android app has been developed for jewellery shopping, offering users an AR (Augmented Reality) try-on experience. This app provides a wide variety of jewellery options, making buying jewellery online easier. The AR technology provides a realistic experience for the users, which helps them make informed decisions while purchasing jewellery.


Smart Dustbin: This project is based on IoT. It is a dustbin that is powered by sensors to work more efficiently. This dustbin is designed to segregate dry waste and wet waste separately. The sensors in the dustbin notify the authorities when the dustbin is full or overweight and needs to be emptied.


Medical Management System: The Python-based medical management system, employing Tkinter for the GUI, facilitates effective medical inventory control, prescription tracking, and streamlined management of pharmaceutical information. The user-friendly interface enhances accessibility for healthcare professionals, optimizing the organization and monitoring of drug-related data.
